The Film Section

Though Clandestine is now complete, we view it as an ongoing project exploring the grey area between fact and fiction. In this section we hope to condense and publish our stories, travels and research as they relate to the broad topic of non-fiction film and the production of Clandestine.

This section is also the best place to find information about the film itself. Take time to explore our Film Details and Cast & Crew sections.

Please check back often or follow our blog, Twitter or Facebook to become aware of new developments.
