Marcus Rosentrater

Bio

After moving to Atlanta in 2004, Marcus quickly got accepted into the local film community by taking on three, long-time abandoned, editing projects. One of which was a project filmed two years prior by Gideon Kennedy. This project provided the most fertile ground for long term creative collaboration. Since then, they have formed a production company, Climenole LLC, and have directed three short films, six commercials, and one music video. These films have played worldwide winning two awards.
