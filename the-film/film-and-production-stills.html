Numbers Stations cyphers pass under foot of American soldiers storming Berlin during WWII in Clandestine.

Clandestine is told by re-purposing various types of free online ephemera, through meaningful visual effects.

The faces of many men are used to represent the father, the spies, and sometimes the narrator in Clandestine.
